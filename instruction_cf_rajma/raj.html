Rajma Chawal

Welcome to our flavorful Rajma Chawal recipe! Rajma Chawal, a popular North Indian dish, consists of red kidney beans cooked in a rich, aromatic gravy served with steamed rice. This comforting and nutritious meal is loved by many for its delicious taste and simplicity.
