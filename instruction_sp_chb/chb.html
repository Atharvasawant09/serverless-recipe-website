Chole Bhature

Indulge in the iconic flavors of Chole Bhature, a popular North Indian dish cherished for its spicy chickpea curry served with fluffy fried bread. This hearty and satisfying meal is a favorite street food and also makes for a delightful homemade treat. Prepare to tantalize your taste buds with the irresistible combination of spicy chickpeas and soft, puffed bread.
